Postgraduate Programmes:

Being an affiliated college of Krishna University, admissions take place on the basis of the ranks obtained in KRU-CET for M.Sc.Courses and the ranks obtained in I-CET for MBA & MCA Courses.

P.G.Courses offered by Andhra Loyola College (As per the rules of University & State Govt.) are:
  1. Master of Computer Applications
  2. Master of Business Administration
  3. M.Sc Botany
  4. M.Sc Physics
  5. M.Sc Maths
  6. .M.Sc Chemistry
  • The academic year consists of two semesters with a system of evaluation based on Continuous Internal Assessment (CIA) and Semester-End Examination (SEE) i.e. internal exams and external exams in a 50:50 ratio as per University norms. Project work is compulsory for all final year students except for M.Sc. Physics.
